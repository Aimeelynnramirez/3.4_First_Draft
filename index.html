Furture to Come?

Some of the biggest changes were caused by the rapid improvement of computer processor speed, hard drive storage space, and internet access speed. (Which increased over 10000% over the course of the last 50 years) As computers became stronger and faster, technologies were given more room to take advantage of these speed improvements. For example, a basic React Hello World demo would destroy a computer from the 90s, but works fine now. Another thing of historical influence was the ubiquity of internet access, meaning how common and easy it was for people to access the internet. As access increased, people of different needs began accessing the internet. (Blind, deaf, etc) This led to many of the changes introduced in HTML5.
